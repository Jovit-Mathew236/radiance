THE QUIDDITCH (Inter)

The proposal is that of a Capture the Flag competition. Through this initiative, we can provide opportunities for beginner and intermediate cyber security aspirants to test their skills. Custom vulnerable machines can be provided to the participants through websites like HackTheBox, VulnHub, and HackThisSite. Participants can be provided with these machines in chronological order depending on their level of difficulty. The first participant to capture all the flags or the participant who captured the most number of flags will be selected as the winner.

Venue: M Tech Lab
Prize Money: 8k
